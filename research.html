Research Experiences

I am interested in reliable learning and computation under privacy, uncertainty, and structural constraints.

2025 - Present

Robust Coreness Estimation under Edge-Level Local Differential Privacy

Developed and analyzed iterative H-index estimators for node coreness from locally randomized graph data, including an adaptive variant that allocates privacy budget according to convergence behavior.

Connected coreness estimation error to approximate k-core recovery guarantees and evaluated accuracy, robustness, and scalability on large real-world graphs.

  • Graph algorithms
  • Local differential privacy
  • k-core decomposition

2026 - Present

Logistic Bandits under Reward-Only Local Differential Privacy

Developed frequentist and Bayesian bandit algorithms that learn from locally randomized binary rewards while preserving regret guarantees for private online recommendation.

Evaluated the methods on MovieLens and Last.fm and studied the trade-off between privacy, information acquisition, and recommendation performance.

  • Contextual bandits
  • Recommendation
  • Bayesian learning

2026 - Present

RepCut for Chip Design Optimization

Studied replication-aware partitioning for chip design and developed structure-aware graph and hypergraph formulations for large-scale partitioning problems.

  • Graph partitioning
  • Hypergraphs
  • Optimization

October 2024

Monochromatic K4 Minimization

Implemented a conditional-expectation algorithm to reduce monochromatic K4 subgraphs in two-colored graphs.

  • Probabilistic method
  • Combinatorics
  • Algorithms
