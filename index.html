Selected research

Questions I am working on.

01

Private graph analytics

Designing robust graph algorithms under local privacy constraints, with a current focus on iterative H-index estimation and approximate k-core recovery.

  • Graph algorithms
  • Local differential privacy
  • k-core
02

Private online recommendation

Developing frequentist and Bayesian logistic bandit methods that learn from randomized, reward-only feedback while retaining meaningful regret guarantees.

  • Contextual bandits
  • Recommendation
  • Statistical learning
03

Structure-aware optimization

Exploring graph and hypergraph formulations for replication-aware partitioning in large-scale chip design and related combinatorial problems.

  • Optimization
  • Hypergraphs
  • Chip design
