Biography

I am an incoming M.S. student in Mathematics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I received my B.S. in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2026.

My research interests include graph data mining, differential privacy, online recommendation, contextual bandits, and statistical learning. I am especially interested in designing methods that remain reliable when observations are noisy, decentralized, or deliberately randomized for privacy.
