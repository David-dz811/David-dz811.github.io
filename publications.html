Publications

My current work focuses on privacy-preserving graph analytics and sequential decision-making with locally randomized feedback.

2027

Robust Coreness Estimation via H-index under Edge-LDP

Zheyuan Dai, Xuliang Zhu, Yizhang He, Kai Wang, Fan Zhang, and Xuemin Lin.

Manuscript under revision for ACM SIGMOD 2027.

We study accurate node-coreness estimation from locally randomized graph data through iterative and adaptive H-index estimators, and connect estimation error to approximate k-core recovery.

Private Clicks, Public Actions: Logistic Bandits under Local Differential Privacy

Zheyuan Dai, Xuliang Zhu, Ruijia Wu, Yizhang He, Gengda Zhao, and Kai Wang.

Submitted to AAAI 2027.

We develop frequentist and Bayesian logistic bandit algorithms for private online recommendation using reward-only randomized feedback.
